Claire Cofelice

Student and Aspiring Designer

Claire Cofelice is currently a sophomore studying communication design and art history at Washington University in St. Louis. After graduating, she hopes to stay in the city and take advantage of its growing arts community by pursuing a career at an interdisciplinary design firm.

When not in St. Louis, Claire lives with her parents, younger sister, and 7-year-old dachshund in a suburb outside of Boston.

In her free time, Claire enjoys hiking, drawing, meditating, and cooking for her friends and family.
