Student

I am currently studying art and design at Washington University in St. Louis. But I am also a student of languages, constellations, and the world. Even after I graduate, I anticipate that I will always be a student, experimenting with new materials, ideas, techniques and of course always making new mistakes.

Creative

I am deeply passionate about the arts. Footage of me weaving textiles one second and surrounded by piles of handmade paper cranes the next fill my family’s earliest home videos. Instead of watching TV or playing video games until I fell asleep, I spent my time styling outfits for imagined fashion shows and submerged in paste as I tried to paper mâché models of my friends (wearing clothes that I'd sewn, of course).

Adventurer

Having grown up at the base of Vermont's Taconic Mountain Range, I have developed a penchant for exploration. I love travelling to new landmarks, cities, and countries, picking up new knowledge along the way.
